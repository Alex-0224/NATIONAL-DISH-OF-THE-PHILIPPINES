Lutong Bahay: Chicken adobo

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.0/5)

Arguable the national dish of the Philippines! Savory, sour, and perfect with warm rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken pieces
  • 1/2 cup of Soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of vinegar
  • 4 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 2 Bay leaves

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Pan-fry the chicken until it's golden brown.
  3. Add the marinade,vinegar, and bay leaves. Simmer it until it's tender.
